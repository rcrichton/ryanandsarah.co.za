Location finding the venue

Calderwood Hall is situated on the R617 just 31km from the Howick turnoff. It is approximately 130km (1 hour 30 minutes) from Durban and 500km (5 hours 15 minutes) from Johannesburg. From the N3 take exit 99 and continue straight on the Underberg road for 31km. Calderwood Hall will be on you right.
